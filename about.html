Who We Are

About
Evalanche

A student community built to push the boundaries of ECE education.

The Evalanche Club is the flagship student organisation of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at Jain (Deemed-to-be) University, Bengaluru.

We believe that education doesn't end in the classroom. Our club exists to give students a space to experiment, collaborate, fail fast, and build the skills that textbooks can't teach — leadership, communication, real-world problem solving, and creative thinking.

From circuit debugging competitions to hackathons, cultural festivals to social outreach — Evalanche is where ECE students discover what they're truly capable of.

Our tagline says it all: "For the students, by the students."
