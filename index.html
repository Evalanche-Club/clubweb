Who We Are:

A community built on curiosity & code

The Evalanche Club is the student-led technical and cultural hub of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at Jain (Deemed-to-be) University. We bridge the gap between classroom knowledge and real-world innovation — through hackathons, seminars, workshops, and competitions that push boundaries.


Our Full Story

Vision

To create a platform where students explore, learn, and showcase their talents — bridging theoretical knowledge with practical application and nurturing the next generation of ECE innovators.

Mission

Enhance technical skills, develop soft skills, foster cultural awareness, encourage networking, and provide a platform for holistic personal and professional growth.

Our Journey

Club Timeline

2020

Evalanche Club Founded

The club was established by a group of ECE students and faculty at Jain University with a vision to build a student-first engineering community from the ground up.

FOUNDING
2021

First Innovathon

Launched our flagship 8-hour innovation challenge. Over 60 students across 15 teams participated in the inaugural edition, marking the start of a tradition.

MILESTONE
2022

24-Hour Hackathon & 200+ Members

Scaled our biggest event to a 24-hour format and crossed the 200-member mark. Introduced the first inter-department cultural event and social service programme.

GROWTH
2023

Website Launch & Industry Workshops

Launched our first digital presence and partnered with industry professionals for hands-on workshops in Embedded Systems, VLSI Design, and IoT.

DIGITAL
2024

500+ Members & Cloudflare-Hosted Site

Crossed 500 active members, ran 40+ events in a single academic year, and migrated to a professional custom domain — this site you're on now.
